_Security_

* [Cookie Monster](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/4703) - Tell it to disallow cookies globally and then you can just enable cookies on a per site basis.
* [Cookiesafe](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2497) - NoScript, but it does cookies. (This is shit compared to the above)
* [BetterPrivacy](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/6623) - BetterPrivacy is a Super-Cookie Safeguard which protects from usually undeletable LSO's (Flash-Cookies) or DOM Storage Objects. It blocks longterm tracking on Google, YouTube Ebay and many other domains. Tell it to remove all LSO's and stuff on exit of firefox.
* [FoxyProxy](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2464) - Fast [proxy](/proxy) switcher
* [NoScript](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/722) - Enable, block scripts globally, and add trusted sites. At first it is tedious but it saves you from unknown dangers.
* [RefControl](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/953) - Click the menu at the bottom, set the default to "Forge"
* [TorButton](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/2275) - [Tor](/Tor) on, Tor off; comes with the Tor Bundle. <- SENDS INFORMATION TO ALEXA!!!
* [User Agent Switcher](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/59) - Changes your [useragent.html Useragent].
